Privacy Policy

BoxIt – Daily Timeboxing  ·  Last updated: June 20, 2026

The short version: BoxIt does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Data We Collect

None. BoxIt does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or any other data from users.

How Your Data is Stored

All data you create in BoxIt — tasks, time blocks, settings, and daily review information — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device.

BoxIt does not sync data to any server or cloud service. BoxIt does not require an account or login of any kind.

Third-Party Services

BoxIt does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. No third-party SDKs in BoxIt collect or share data.

Permissions

BoxIt requests the following device permissions. All are optional — denying any permission does not break the core app.

PermissionWhyIf denied
AlarmKit Hard-stop alarm when a time block ends, even on silent mode Alarm silenced; a standard notification is shown as fallback
Notifications Buffer nudges and end-of-day review reminders Reminders suppressed; core app fully functional
Live Activities Countdown on Lock Screen and Dynamic Island Live Activity not shown; countdown visible inside the app

BoxIt does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, photos, or Health data.

Changes to This Policy

If a future version of BoxIt ever adds network features or data collection, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will change. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
